WATER FILTER


materials

  • 1 Kg of fine sand
  • 1 Kg of gravel
  • 1 Kg of small rocks
  • 1 Kg of medium rocks
  • 1/2 Kg of shredded coal or activated coal
Homemade water filter: The process to do this water filter is simple. The materials must be put in order,
because each material have a function in the Project. In the upper part of the wáter filter
must be used a tube that comes out below, and this part Will be transparent to be able to
see how much wáter passes through the filter, if the wáter is at the same height in the tube
than in the filter, means that the filter is wrongly armed but if is a Little bit lower the filter is
properly armed. Is not necesary but must be helpful. It has a bacterial function with activated
coal and a good taste of wáter.
Filter layers:
  • -15 cm of médium rocks
  • -10 cm of small rocks
  • -3 cm of gravel
  • -3 cm of activated coal
  • -2 cm of gravel
  • -5 cm of fine sand
  • - 10 cm of small rocks


Operation and maintenance of the wáter filter: The beginning is simple, the wáter passes by
the upper part of the filter, through the different layers to reach the end. Principally must be
thought with the purpose of distributing purified wáter. You can even make a quick filter with only a bottle
